Welcome!

In this exercise you are called to evaluate a new search engine (Re-Search Engine). In the next pages, you will be asked a series of questions. Please use the Re-Search Engine, provided right below the question, in order to find the answers to those questions. This way, you will have a chance to experience the Re-Search Engine and evaluate its performance.

At the end of this searching exercise you will be asked to answer a brief questionnaire on your Internet use. This will help us in providing a better service.

The whole test will take approximately 30 minutes.

Please ensure that you have a reliable Internet connection before starting the test. If you abandon the test before the end, you will not be paid.

Thank you for your collaboration!

In order to participate in this study, you must be 18 years old or older, you must be connecting from the U.S.A., you must have at least 30 uninterrupted minutes to complete the study, and at the moment you must be using a reliable Internet connection that will make it possible for you to complete the study.

You will also be asked some non-mandatory questions about personal behaviors.
